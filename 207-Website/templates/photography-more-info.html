Photography

Course: Photography

Status: Open

Duration: 60 minutes

Type: Live

Created: 17/08/2023

Cost: Free

With our thorough online course, you may embark on an exciting adventure into the world of photography. This course, designed for both beginners and experts, will teach you the art and science of generating spectacular visual narratives. You'll learn the foundations of composition, lighting, and camera settings from seasoned photographers.

Course Highlights:

  • Recognising camera types and settings
  • Investigating composition and framing methods
  • Understanding natural and artificial lighting
  • Navigating multiple photography genres
  • Post-processing and picture editing basics
  • Creating a personal photographic style
  • Practical exercises and peer reviews
  • Creating a compelling image portfolio

Time: Wednesday 1-3pm
